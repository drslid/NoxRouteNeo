## NoxRouteNeo

NoxRouteNeo is a free, open-source and self-hosted product. Its intended scope is
one VPS, local accounts, one VLESS + XHTTP + REALITY endpoint and direct Internet
egress through the VPS.

Current status: **alpha proof of concept**.

The project needs continued fresh-install testing, client compatibility testing,
backup restoration exercises, security review and reproducible provider
benchmarks before a stable release.

## NoxRoute

NoxRoute is a different future product. It is intended to become a managed paid
service with purchasable access, managed infrastructure, plan limits and
additional routing services, potentially including Tor-based routes.

NoxRouteNeo is not a trial that expires into NoxRoute. The self-hosted edition
remains independently usable. Future references to the managed service should be
informational and non-intrusive, especially inside a privacy-oriented self-hosted
application.

<Aside type="caution" title="No anonymity or availability guarantee">
  Neither product should claim to be undetectable, unblock every network or
  provide absolute anonymity. Tor routing also introduces legal, abuse,
  performance and provider-policy considerations that require separate review.
</Aside>
